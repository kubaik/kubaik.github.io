4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us improve your experience, understand how you use our Site, and deliver relevant content and advertisements.

4.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic site functionality Session
Analytics Cookies Track site usage and performance Up to 2 years
Advertising Cookies Deliver relevant advertisements Up to 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences Up to 1 year

4.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our Site. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer.

4.4 Other Tracking Technologies

  • Web Beacons: Small graphic images used in emails and web pages to track engagement
  • Pixel Tags: Used to measure advertising campaign effectiveness
  • Local Storage: Browser storage used to save preferences and settings
