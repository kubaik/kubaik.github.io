How Pricing Actually Works Under the Hood

Pricing a SaaS product involves understanding the underlying costs and revenue streams. One key concept is the customer lifetime value (CLV), which represents the total revenue a customer is expected to generate over their lifetime. To calculate CLV, you need to estimate the average revenue per user (ARPU) and the customer retention rate. For instance, if your ARPU is $50 per month and your customer retention rate is 90%, your CLV would be around $500. Another important concept is the cost of acquisition (COA), which represents the cost of acquiring a new customer. This can include marketing and sales expenses, as well as the cost of providing support and onboarding.
